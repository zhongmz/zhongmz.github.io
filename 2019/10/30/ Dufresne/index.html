Dufresne

“It was very bitter.”

“She said she was glad i knew,that she hated all the sneaking around. And she said tha she wanted a divorce in Reno.”

“I told her I would not grant one.”

“If they say so.”

“I really don’t remember. I was upset.”

“She packed a bag…She packed a bag to go and stay with Mr.Quentin.”

“I went to a few bars first. Later,I drove to his house to confront them. They weren’t home. So i parked in the turnout…and waited.”

“I am not sure,I was confused, and drunk.”

“I think mostly I wanted to scare them.”

“No,I was sobering up.”

“I got back in the car and I drove home to sleep it off. “

“Along the way,I threw my gun into the Royal River. I’ve been very clear on this point.”

“Yes, it does. “

“It’s the truth.”

“Since I am innocent of this crime. I find it decidedly inconvenient that the gun was never found.”
